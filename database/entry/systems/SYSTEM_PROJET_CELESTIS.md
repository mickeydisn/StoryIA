# [[SYSTEM_PROJET_CELESTIS]] - Projet Celestis (Heavenly Preservation)

## Basic Information
- **System Name**: Projet Celestis (Celestial Archive Project)
- **Type**: Orbital Data Preservation and Human Continuity System
- **Category**: Infrastructure/Societal Preservation
- **Scientific Basis**: Orbital mechanics, cryogenics, long-term data storage, human life support systems
- **Core Technology**: Lagrange point habitat construction, autonomous orbital infrastructure, AI-managed archival systems
- **Scale**: Planetary-to-solar system scale
- **Complexity Level**: Extreme
- **Limitations**: Massive energy requirements, construction timeline constraints, terrestrial dependency during build phase

## Technical Specifications
- **Scientific Basis**: Orbital mechanics and sustainable life support for extended human presence
- **Core Technology**: Modular habitat construction, autonomous assembly systems, quantum-encrypted archival storage
- **Scale**: Multi-platform orbital network
- **Complexity Level**: Extreme
- **Limitations**: Construction vulnerability to terrestrial conflicts, energy requirements, human psychological factors

## Capabilities & Functions
- **Primary Function**: Preserve human knowledge, culture, and biological continuity beyond terrestrial threats
- **Secondary Functions**:
  - Autonomous data archival and retrieval
  - Human life support in orbital environment
  - Emergency evacuation and continuity protocols
  - Cultural preservation through living communities
  - Scientific research continuity

- **Performance Metrics**: 99.9% data integrity preservation; life support for 10,000+ inhabitants; operational autonomy for 50+ years
- **Limitations**:
  - Dependent on terrestrial supply chains during construction
  - Psychological strain on orbital inhabitants
  - Vulnerability to orbital debris and solar events
  - Economic burden on terrestrial societies

## Operational Details
- **Activation/Control**: Initiated 2031 following bit-famine predictions; autonomous AI oversight with human governance
- **Maintenance Requirements**: Regular orbital debris management, habitat integrity monitoring, resource recycling systems
- **Energy/Power Needs**: Solar arrays, nuclear thermal propulsion, beamed power from terrestrial sources
- **Failure Modes**:
  - Construction interruption by terrestrial conflicts
  - Habitat breach from micrometeorites
  - AI system corruption or misalignment
  - Resource depletion in isolation
