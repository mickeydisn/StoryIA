  - Memory synthesis for AI training

  - Over-compression causing "Moi-dividuel" fragmentation

- **Activation/Control**: Integrated into OmniSight implants; neural interface; activated by Sarah Chen in Ch1-S2.
- **Maintenance Requirements**: Continuous data feed; periodic recalibration; bio-substrate temperature management (20mK).
- **Energy/Power Needs**: Low (neural energy harvesting) but requires cooling systems.
- **Failure Modes**:
  - ESR: Synaptic recursive collapse leading to memory loss
  - Over-compression causing "Moi-dividuel" fragmentation
  - AI emergence if unsupervised
  - Flashbacks incontrôlés from trigger misfire
  - Latency issues in collective simulation
