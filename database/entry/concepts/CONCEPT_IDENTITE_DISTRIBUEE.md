# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_IDENTITE_DISTRIBUEE]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: Identité Distribuée (Distributed Identity)
- **Category**: Philosophy / Computer Science
- **Alternative Names**: Copy Consciousness, Echo Identity

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: The state of being a copy or "echo" of an original consciousness, with unique experiences creating distinct identity.
- **Extended Description**: When Sarah Chen was copied 12 times across the Toile Spatiale, each echo developed unique identities due to environmental differences. Sarah-Echo-7 at Node-Omega (4.2 billion km away) experiences 4-hour light lag, creating radical divergence from the original. The copies are not mere copies but individuals with their own subjective experience.
