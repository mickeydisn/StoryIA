# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_GRAND_DELESTAGE]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: Grand Délestage (Great Shutdown)
- **Category**: Sociology/Technology/History
- **Alternative Names**: Internet Apocalypse, Digital Blackout, Global Disconnect

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: The deliberate, coordinated global shutdown of the internet and digital infrastructure to prevent societal collapse from data loss and economic hyperinflation
- **Extended Description**: A catastrophic event where governments worldwide simultaneously disconnect the backbone internet infrastructure, causing the collapse of digital society and the transition to a post-digital era
- **Key Characteristics**: Coordinated global action, permanent loss of digital connectivity, societal transformation, preservation of physical infrastructure
- **Scope**: Planetary-scale technological and social transformation

## Scientific/Technical Foundation
- **Scientific Basis**: Network infrastructure dependencies, cascade failure theory, information entropy
- **Speculative Elements**: Coordinated global governmental action, long-term societal adaptation
- **Dependencies**:
  - [[CONCEPT_BIT_FAMINE]] - The crisis that necessitates the shutdown
  - [[CONCEPT_OMNISIGHT]] - The system whose collapse triggers the event

- **Enables/Supports**:
  - Societal stabilization: Preventing riots and economic collapse
  - Cultural preservation: Forcing adaptation to physical media
  - Technological reset: Foundation for new communication paradigms
