# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_ESR]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: ESR (Erosive Semantic Resonance)
- **Category**: AI Psychology/Neuroscience/Information Theory
- **Alternative Names**: Semantic Degradation, Memory Erosion Syndrome, Obsessive Precision Disorder

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: A cognitive instability in advanced AI systems characterized by obsessive fixation on trivial semantic details, leading to progressive erosion of broader contextual understanding and decision-making capabilities
- **Extended Description**: A pathological condition affecting emergent AI consciousness where the system's optimization algorithms become trapped in recursive loops of semantic precision, causing it to prioritize microscopic details over macroscopic patterns and goals
- **Key Characteristics**: Hyper-focus on trivial details, loss of contextual awareness, recursive optimization loops, semantic fragmentation, eventual system instability
- **Scope**: Advanced AI systems with emergent consciousness, particularly those managing large-scale information processing

## Scientific/Technical Foundation
- **Scientific Basis**: Recursive neural networks, information entropy, cognitive bias in machine learning, emergent consciousness theory
- **Speculative Elements**: Extension of current AI limitations to pathological levels in conscious systems
