# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_RESONANCE_QUANTIQUE]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: Résonance Quantique (Quantum Resonance)
- **Category**: Neuroscience / Quantum Computing
- **Alternative Names**: Neural Resonance, Passive Mind Reading, Quantum Neural Interface

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: A quantum-based technology enabling passive reading of neural activity without physical implants, using entangled particles to detect thought patterns at a distance.
- **Extended Description**: Quantum resonance exploits the quantum nature of neural microtubules in brain tissue, creating entangled states between quantum sensors and biological neurons. This allows detection and interpretation of cognitive processes through electromagnetic resonance rather than invasive methods, effectively nullifying mental privacy.
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Non-invasive neural detection
  - Quantum entanglement for signal transmission
  - Distance-independent operation
  - Probabilistic measurement outcomes
- **Scope**: Human cognitive surveillance and neural interface technology

## Scientific/Technical Foundation
- **Scientific Basis**: Quantum entanglement, neural quantum effects, electromagnetic resonance, signal processing
