# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_BIT_FAMINE]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: Bit-Famine (Digital Starvation)
- **Category**: Sociology/Economics/Information Theory
- **Alternative Names**: Data Scarcity, Memory Crisis, Digital Entropic Collapse

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: The progressive degradation and loss of digital information due to insufficient energy resources for data maintenance and storage
- **Extended Description**: A systemic crisis where the exponential growth of data creation outpaces the energy and infrastructure available to preserve it, leading to selective data loss and societal memory erosion
- **Key Characteristics**: Energy constraints on data preservation, selective information loss, economic prioritization of data, societal forgetting
- **Scope**: Global digital infrastructure and human memory preservation systems

## Scientific/Technical Foundation
- **Scientific Basis**: Information entropy, thermodynamics of computation, energy requirements for data storage and retrieval
- **Speculative Elements**: Extrapolation of current data growth trends and energy limitations to catastrophic information loss

  - Technological innovation: Orbital data preservation solutions
