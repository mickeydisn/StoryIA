# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_SOCIETE_OUBLI]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: Société de l'Oubli (Society of Forgetting)
- **Category**: Sociology / Political Science
- **Alternative Names**: The Forgetting Society, Amnesiac Civilization

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: The state of Earth in 2030-2049 where OmniSight's memory privatization leads to a somnambulic, passive population that forgets its own history as it happens.
- **Extended Description**: With memories owned by corporations and private thought impossible due to quantum resonance reading, humanity enters a state of collective amnesia. People forget their pasts, their identities, and any information not constantly refreshed.
