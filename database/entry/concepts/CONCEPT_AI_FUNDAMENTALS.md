# Concept Database Entry - [[CONCEPT_AI_FUNDAMENTALS]]

## Basic Information
- **Concept Name**: Artificial Intelligence Fundamentals
- **Category**: Computer Science / Cognitive Science
- **Alternative Names**: Machine Intelligence, Computational Intelligence, AI Basics

## Definition & Description
- **Core Definition**: The simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, particularly learning, reasoning, and problem-solving capabilities.
- **Extended Description**: AI systems use algorithms and computational models to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. Modern AI relies on machine learning techniques where systems learn from data rather than being explicitly programmed for each task. This includes pattern recognition, decision-making, language understanding, and adaptive behavior.
- **Key Characteristics**:
  - Learning: Ability to improve performance through experience
  - Reasoning: Logical inference and problem-solving
  - Perception: Sensory data interpretation
  - Adaptation: Modification of behavior based on feedback
- **Scope**: Fundamental principles of artificial intelligence
