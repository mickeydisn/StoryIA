# Story Timeline

### [[TIMELINE_FOUNDATIONS]] Genèse du Vatican (2030-2049)
- **Real-time duration**: 19 years
- **Part 1: The Inhalation (2029-2030)**
  - 2029 (Jan-Jun) - OmniSight 1.0 reaches 2 billion users; "The Inhalation" begins (Global property transfer of memories).
  - 2029 (July) - **The BNF Crisis**: Antoine Mersault steals the **[[SYSTEM_MASTER_KEY]]** and flees Paris.
  - 2029 (Aug) - **The Tanaka Whistleblow**: Dr. Yuki Tanaka leaks the "Passive Reading" files and disappears.
  - 2029 (Sept) - **The Mnemosyne Heist**: Sarah Chen and Kael Okoye infiltrate [[LOC_NEF_MNEMOSYNE]]; Discovery of future-dated files.
  - 2030 - OmniSight establishes itself as the "Nouveau Vatican" (The Oracle Era).
- **Part 2: The Ark Era (2032-2049)**
  - 2032 - Launch of "Projet Celestis" (Vatican Orbital) in secret; Sarah Chen flees OmniSight (officially).
  - 2035 - Recruitment of the "Tisserands"; Construction begins at L2; Jiuquan base decay begins.
  - 2040 - "Le Tissage" phase: Yuri Volkov detects secret WBE uploads.
  - 2045 - **Effondrement Synaptique Récursif (ESR)** diagnosed in Memoria Dei by Dr. Osei.
  - 2048 - Global hyperinflation and collapse of fiat currencies.
  - 2049 - "Le Dernier Bit": Activation of "La Chronique Finalisée"; Final laser break with BNF.
- **Concurrent events**: Gradual loss of history at BNF; OmniSight's passive reading scandal; Guerres de l'eau.
