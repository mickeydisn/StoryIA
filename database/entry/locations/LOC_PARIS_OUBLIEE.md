# Location Database Entry - [[LOC_PARIS_OUBLIEE]]

## Basic Information
- **Location Name**: Paris-Oubliée (Forgotten Paris)
- **Type**: Reclaimed Ruins / Forest City / Post-Apocalyptic Wilderness
- **Scale**: City-wide (former 2.2 million population area, now overgrown wilderness)

## Physical Description
- **Environment**: Overgrown urban ruins; tropicalized atmosphere from elevated CO2 and temperature
- **Climate/Atmosphere**: High O2 levels (28%); lush vegetation; eerily silent except for natural sounds
- **Gravity**: Standard Earth gravity
- **Radiation**: Background radiation from collapsed nuclear facilities
- **Geographical Features**: Notre Dame cathedral overgrown with mega-trees; Eiffel Tower serving as trellis for climbing vines; Bibliothèque Nationale de France transformed into sacred cave; Seine River choked with vegetation; Arc de Triomphe partially buried under soil

## Technical Specifications
- **Power Sources**: None functional (solar panels overgrown)
- **Life Support**: Natural ecosystem provides oxygen; no artificial climate control
- **Transportation**: Foot travel only; overgrown paths through ruins
- **Communications**: None (electromagnetic interference from solar activity)
- **Defenses/Security**: Natural camouflage; dangerous wildlife; unstable structures

## Societal & Population
- **Inhabitants**:

  - **Demographics**: Human survivors adapted to wilderness; mutated wildlife species
  - **Social Structure**: Tribal survival groups; no centralized society
- **Culture/Atmosphere**: Primal, spiritual, nature-reverent; blend of human remnants and natural dominance
- **Economy**: Barter-based survival economy; no formal currency

## History & Development
- **Origin**: Former Paris, France metropolitan area; abandoned during Grand Délestage
- **Evolution**:
  - **Phase 1 (2049-2100)**: Initial abandonment; gradual overgrowth begins
  - **Phase 2 (2100-2500)**: Tropicalization from climate change; mega-flora development
  - **Phase 3 (2500-3000)**: Sacred site formation; human survivors develop spiritual reverence
  - **Phase 4 (3000-present)**: Panspermia Narrative integration; Architecture Vivante deployment
- **Current Status**: Living monument to human civilization's fall and nature's reclamation
- **Future Plans**: Continued integration with Panspermia Narrative; potential human resettlement
