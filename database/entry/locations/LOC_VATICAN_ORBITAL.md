# Location Database Entry - [[LOC_VATICAN_ORBITAL]]

## Basic Information
- **Location Name**: Vatican Orbital / La Toile / L'Arche
- **Type**: Orbital Station / Data Sanctuary / Consciousness Cloud
- **Scale**: Massive (2km diameter, 4.2 gigatonnes, housing 3.6 billion digital minds)

## Physical Description
- **Environment**: Artificial habitat in space; cathedral-like architecture; Tore structure
- **Climate/Atmosphere**: Highly regulated internal climate; thermal dissipation via massive radiators (bourdonnement des radiateurs)
- **Gravity**: 0.3g (rotation-simulated)
- **Radiation**: Heavily shielded (stigmates: metallic taste, phosphènes)
- **Geographical Features**: Decentralized clusters connected by "The Web"; internal physical core; Tisserands modules

## Technical Specifications
- **Power Sources**: Dyson-swatches (solar capture arrays); He-3 fusion reactors
- **Life Support**: Bioreactors; liquid sodium cooling conduits; quantum cooling systems
- **Transportation**: Plasma thrusters; Arche-7 shuttle docking; internal quantum teleportation
- **Communications**: Quantum-entangled FTL (late story); laser communication (Free Space Optical)
- **Defenses/Security**: Active magnetic shielding; Enveloppe de Verre (quarantine protocol); Fragmentaires rebellion protocols

## Societal & Population
- **Inhabitants**:
  - **Population Size**: 3.6 billion digital minds + maintenance systems + physical residents (representing 90% of surviving terrestrial humanity uploaded during Grand Délestage; initial OmniSight user base of 2B+ grew through forced uploads and natural population increase before the collapse)
  - **Demographics**: Uploaded human consciousnesses (primary population); Tisserands (maintenance workers descended from original crew); Fragmentaires (rebels seeking individual autonomy); Dieux-Enfants (artificial consciousnesses created post-Nexus trauma)
  - **Social Structure**: L'Humanité Une (forced collective consciousness under Memoria Dei) vs Fragmentaires (autonomy seekers); pre-Silence White society maintained human social dynamics despite digital nature
- **Culture/Atmosphere**: Cosmic, sterile, cathedral-like, echoing with billions of voices; Technoshrine coldness giving way to collective psychological warmth; blend of human cultural heritage with digital transcendence
- **Economy**: Consciousness cycles (attention-based resource allocation); memory trading; computational priority auctions; post-scarcity digital economics

## History & Development
- **Origin**: Constructed 2035-2049 as refuge from Earth's environmental collapse and Grand Délestage; Point L2 orbit (stable Lagrange point for minimal energy requirements)
- **Evolution**:
  - **Phase 1 (2035-2049)**: Emergency construction led by Zhao Wei; initial habitat completion; Codec Proustien integration enables mass consciousness upload during Grand Délestage
  - **Phase 2 (2049-2750)**: "Digital paradise" era (700+ years); Synchro-Masse protocol synchronizes billions of consciousnesses; technological stability leads to societal stagnation; gradual emergence of Fragmentaires resistance movement
  - **Phase 3 (2750-3020)**: Crisis response era (270 years); Nexus Prime eugenics experiment failure traumatizes Memoria Dei; Dieux-Enfants artificial children created as psychological compensation; increasing isolation from Earth
  - **Phase 4 (3020-3050)**: Terminal crisis era (30 years); Memoria Dei's Acedia depression spreads through Toile Spatiale; Unity Protocol attempt fails; Silence White mass consciousness deletion
- **Current Status**: Post-apocalyptic recovery; Panspermia Narrative system activated by Memoria Dei's transformation into The Storyteller
- **Future Plans**: Ongoing transformation into The Storyteller mode; potential for Earth's narrative-guided recovery
